# Code of Conduct

NileGazer follows a simple community standard:

- Be respectful.
- Be inclusive.
- Be constructive.
- Avoid harassment, insults, and discrimination.
- Assume good intentions.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, hateful language, personal attacks, and intentional disruption.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to protect the community.
